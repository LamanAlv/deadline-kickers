HOUR OF CODE

Our Hour of Code took a place approximately at 5 o’clock on 8th December. Due to the on-going situation with the pandemic, it was decided to make this lesson on-line. Thus, twenty pupils from the seventh grade of Narimanov District School-Lyceum Complex attended our lesson. At the very beginning, each of us firstly introduced him or herself, and then we told about the ADA University, its faculties and, the number of possibilities that they can acquire here.It can be said that the lesson went quite gradually since the students were very active and willingly absorbed new information. They were already familiar with the concept of algorithms and how the code is structured.

We provided children with additional information about algorithms and explained how each of them works. Our group cited the tasks from Code.org as an example, which was very effective in teaching since it was both entertaining and convenient for students to understand the principle of coding. Students were able to apply acquired knowledge in practice on the tasks of Classic Maze Puzzle from the site Code.org. Encouraged by the fact they can get a certificate on finishing the course, they were intensively completing the task. To avoid any misconception, each member of our group explained all levels of the course. Students were happy to get such an experience, and we are grateful to Esmira Quliyeva and Sevil Xemmedova, who provided us with a class of active and responsive kids.
